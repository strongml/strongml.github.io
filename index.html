About Us

Customer Data 

Real world customer data is dirty, messy, and often wrong. Cleaning this data is tedious and error-prone. The key is to automate data cleanup while breaking down data silos to accurately measure and analyze customer behavior across the organization. 

Funded by private capital, we are a team of National Taiwan University alumni dedicated to the idea that data-driven decision making can improve how companies deliver value and respond to customers. Our mission is to grow and empower the knowledge economy.
